I am a 5th year PhD student (beginning May 2015) from MDO lab in University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. I am fortunate enough to be advised by Prof. Martins. I enjoy doing research in the computational science -- especially solving PDE and optimization problem. I have a strong interest for both the mathematical formulation and its implementation (check our research philosophy in MDO lab). I have been doing research on flutter constrained aircraft MDAO (my thesis work); mixed-integer convex programming formulations for structural optimization. Recently, I spend some time on applying machine learning to our airfoil data set. I contributed to the underlying neural network for the airfoil surrogate model .

Research interests:

  • MDO
  • Aeroelasticity (espicially flutter)
  • Structural optimization
  • Mixed-integer programming
  • Machine learning
  • Laminar--turbulence transition flow modeling
